About me

First off, I am a Web Developer. I say that because not too long ago, I made a decision that would change the trajectory of my future. I spent several years in the Financial Services industry while attending and graduating college with a Bachelors in Business Management. Then, in 2017 I decided things had to change. That change was relocating to Florida and transitioning into a new field.

I spent the next 3 years working diligently and determined to help within the Human Services field. Then, abruptly everything came to a halt, in February of 2020 and I found myself laid off. This was the moment when I discovered, what would become my new passion, Web Development. I proceeded to work day in, day out to learn, practice and develope as much I could...

I have learned the fundamentals of Web Development including HTML, CSS, Javascript; plus modern libraries and frameworks. I have a foundation in Back-End development including servers with Node.js/Express.js and databases in SQL with PostgreSQL. I am proficient with Git and Github, Heroku and Netlify deployment. To Summarise, I am a Web Developer!
